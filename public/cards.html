Illuminate Festival

Produced by: Corbenya Disciplines

Music Festival

Neon Lights

Young adults (ages 18-30) who enjoy electronic dance music (EDM) and vibrant visual experiences

Our Purpose: To create an unforgettable festival experience that showcases top electronic dance music artists and cutting-edge visual effects.

  • Date: August 20-22, 2023
  • Time: 4:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  • Location: The Park at Riverfront, Downtown
  • Details: Food vendors, photo booths, and art installations will be available throughout the festival.
Brand Identity: The festival's visual identity is inspired by neon lights and modern design. The color palette includes bright pink, blue, and purple. The typography is sleek and modern, with the use of sans-serif fonts. The design elements include neon light shapes and gradients that create an electric and futuristic atmosphere.
